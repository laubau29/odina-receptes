Pizza Recipe

Description

Homemade pizza is a delicious and customizable treat that beats store-bought pizza any day. Making your own dough and sauce allows you to create a pizza tailored to your taste, using fresh ingredients.

Ingredients

  • For the Pizza Dough:
  • - 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • - 1 teaspoon granulated sugar
  • - 3/4 cup warm water (110°F or 43°C)
  • - 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • - 1 teaspoon salt
  • - 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • For the Pizza Sauce:
  • - 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • - 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • - 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • - 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • - Salt and pepper to taste
  • For Toppings:
  • - Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • - Sliced pepperoni
  • - Sliced bell peppers
  • - Sliced mushrooms
  • - Sliced black olives

Steps

  1. Prepare the Pizza Dough:
  2. - In a small bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until foamy.
  3. - In a large mixing bowl, mix flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil. Stir until a dough forms.
  4. - Knead the dough on a floured surface for 5-7 minutes until it's smooth and elastic. Place it in a greased bowl, cover, and let it rise for about 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  5. Prepare the Pizza Sauce:
  6. - In a saucepan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  7. - Add crushed tomatoes, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10-15 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  8. Assemble and Bake the Pizza:
  9. - Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C) or as high as it will go.
  10. - Roll out the pizza dough to your desired thickness on a floured surface.
  11. - Transfer the dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet. Spread the homemade sauce over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
  12. - Add a generous layer of shredded mozzarella cheese and your favorite toppings.
  13. - Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  14. - Remove from the oven, let it cool for a moment, slice, and enjoy your homemade pizza!
