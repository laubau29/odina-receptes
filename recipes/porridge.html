Porridge Recipe

Description

This is a healthy and comforting porridge recipe, perfect for breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of rolled oats
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1/4 cup of honey

Steps

  1. Combine oats and milk in a saucepan.
  2. Bring to a simmer and cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Remove from heat, stir in honey, and serve hot.
