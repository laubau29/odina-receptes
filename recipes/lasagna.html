Lasagna Recipe

Description

This delicious lasagna is a classic Italian dish that combines layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy b√©chamel, and melted cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium-high heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add chopped onions and minced garlic to the skillet. Cook until onions are translucent.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes and simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta cheese and shredded mozzarella cheese.
