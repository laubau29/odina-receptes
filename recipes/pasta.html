Pasta Recipe

Description

This is a delicious pasta recipe that you can make in no time!

Ingredients

  • 200g of pasta
  • 2 cups of tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup of sliced mushrooms (fried)
  • 1 cup of fresh spinach leaves (sautéed)

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta until it's al dente.
  2. Heat the tomato sauce in a pan.
  3. While the sauce is heating, sauté the mushrooms until golden brown.
  4. Add the fried mushrooms and sautéed spinach to the sauce.
  5. Drain the pasta and add it to the sauce.
  6. Sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese and serve.
